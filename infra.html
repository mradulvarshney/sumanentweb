Our Infrastructure

Discover the heart of our aromatic expertise at the Distillation Unit of Suman Enterprises. Situated within our state-of-the-art facility, this unit stands as a pinnacle of precision and innovation in the production of flavours, essential oils and mint products. Harnessing centuries-old distillation techniques alongside modern advancements, our Unit transforms nature's gifts into exceptional flavorful and aromatic creations. Here, fresh raw materials undergo a meticulous distillation process, extracting the pure flavor and essence that characterizes our diverse range of flavors and essential oils. Our team of skilled workforce and technicians uphold the highest standards as they orchestrate this intricate process.

Through various processes, we capture the flavor and essence, resulting in a distillate which is rich in flavor and aroma and a symphony of other beneficial compounds. Our processes adhere to stringent quality control measures, ensuring that every product leaving our premises carries the hallmark of Suman Enterprises' commitment to excellence. Our unit is more than just a production facility. It's a hub of creativity, innovation, and collaboration. Our team of experts constantly explores new avenues to enhance our products, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the flavor industry. As you step into our Unit, you step into a world where tradition harmonizes with technology, where every drop is a testament to our dedication to purity and quality. Join us on this aromatic journey as we craft nature's treasures into the essence that defines Suman Enterprises.
