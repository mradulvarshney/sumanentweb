Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Suman Enterprises uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By engaging with our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.


  1. What Are Cookies: Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your device and store information about your preferences and activities.


  2. Types of Cookies Used:
    1. Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and are deleted from your device when you close your browser.
    2. Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a specified period or until you manually delete them.
    3. First-Party Cookies: These cookies are set by the website you are visiting.
    4. Third-Party Cookies: These cookies are set by third-party domains, such as analytics or advertising providers.


  3. How We Use Cookies:
    1. Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the functioning of our website and enable basic features such as navigation and access to secure areas.
    2. Analytical Cookies: We use these cookies to gather information about user behavior on our website, which helps us improve our services.
    3. Functional Cookies: These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences.
    4. Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to display targeted ads based on your browsing habits.


  4. Managing Cookies: You can control and manage cookies by adjusting your browser settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies. However, blocking or deleting cookies may impact your experience on our website and some features may not function properly.


  5. Third-Party Cookies: Our website may contain content from third-party websites, such as social media plugins or embedded videos. These third-party websites may use cookies and collect data. We do not control their use of cookies.


  6. Updates to Cookies Policy: We may update this Cookies Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. By using our website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Cookies Policy.
